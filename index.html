NCSU Senior Design Capstone 2025–26

APOLLO STAR III

My team and I constructed STAR (Seed Transport And Radio Relay) — an autonomous 25 lb 10 ft VTOL UAS designed for modularity and mission readiness for the forestry service. The aircraft supports three flight configurations: 10 ft wingspan, 6 ft wingspan, and multirotor (after removing wing extensions and the tail). Equipped with 1U and 2U payload bays, STAR can suit a wide variety of missions.

  • Radio Repeater Mission

    With the 10 ft wingspan, STAR can hover over someone blocked by terrain, drop a radio via the 2U bay, and loiter at low speeds with a 1U repeater module — enabling communication within a 20-mile radius to determine resources needed and urgency of assistance.

  • Seed Dispersal Mission

    With the 6 ft wingspan, STAR uses a higher-speed flight envelope to maximize terrain coverage. The 2U seed dispersal module drops seeds through an opening at the bottom of the fuselage.

  • Surveillance Mission

    In hover configuration, STAR's low moment of inertia enables precise maneuvering through forested terrain. Live-adjustable onboard video provides multi-angle situational awareness.

Unique Design Solutions

STAR is designed to be a universal solution for any mission-dependent aeronautic need. Each system addresses a specific operational challenge.

Range

Two 6s4p 736 Wh custom Li-Ion packs with live BMS and coulomb counting — 360 A continuous draw and approximately 2 hours of efficient flight.

Stability

2U payload sits at the aircraft CG so payload drops don't shift balance. Wing bracket provides 5° dihedral for sideslip and spiral stability. Autopilot failsafe switches to hover on unexpected attitude exceedance.

Size

Quick-disconnect detent pins allow wings and tail removal, collapsing STAR into a 4′ × 3′ × 2′ enclosure for transport.

Power

VTOL motors produce 41 lbs combined thrust; forward-flight motor produces 17 lbs. Both packs combined allow full motor tandem output. Single-pack failure still permits hover in the 10 ft configuration.
